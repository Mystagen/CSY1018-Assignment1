Software Engineering

(Noun)

The act of designing, building and continuing the upkeep of software

Why I do it

I am studying this because of the satisfaction that can be gained from successfully creating a piece of software after dedicating time to it. The fact that you are able to see it grow as you work on it is worth all the time you have put into it.
